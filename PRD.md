> name. brand. domain: **a16zee** (a-sixteen-ZEE).
> a homophone of a16z, with a vibrating tail.

Built for the builders who don't need a 10-week "curriculum" to figure out how to git push.

---

## 1. Product Vision

**a16zee** is the antithesis of the venture capital industrial complex. While they offer "Speedruns" with workshops on "finding your narrative," we offer the **Cocaine Run**: a high-latency-free environment where the only metric is the frequency of your heart rate and your commits.

## 2. Target Audience

- Founders who have been "vibrating" since they were six.
- Engineers who view "mentorship" as a bottleneck.
- Anyone who realizes that 2026 AI is mostly just overpriced API wrappers and wants to ship something that actually **does something**.

## 3. Core Features & User Flow

### A. The Landing Page (Plaintext Brutalism)

- **Visuals:** No SVG animations. No glassmorphism. No stock photos of diverse people pointing at a tablet. Just black text on a `#FFFFFF` background.
- **The Hook:** "a16z has a Speedrun. We have a Cocaine Run. If you aren't vibrating, you're moving too slow."
- **The Tag:** Must include `#vcevil` in the header.

### B. The Application Form (The "Filter")

A single-page form designed to look high-stakes but functionally irrelevant.

- **Field 1:** GitHub handle (Mandatory. No repos = No entry).
- **Field 2:** "What is your earned secret? (Max 20 words. If you use the word 'disrupt', you're disqualified)."
- **Field 3:** "Current vibration level (1-10)."
- **Field 4:** "How many hours since your last deploy?"
- **CTA Button:** `SHIPPING NOW` (Primary action).

### C. The Redirection (The "Outcome")

- Upon clicking `SHIPPING NOW`, the site performs a fake "analyzing your stack..." progress bar for exactly 1.5 seconds (to simulate AI "reasoning").
- **Trigger:** Immediate redirect to `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ`.

## 4. Technical Requirements

- **Framework:** None. Pure HTML/CSS. If it's over 50kb, it's bloated.
- **AI Beliefs Integration:** The form should include a sarcastic tooltip stating: *"Our 'AI' agent is currently auditing your code for local-first compliance and deterministic reasoning. (Actually, we're just checking if you're a mid)."*
- **External Links:** A tiny, 8px font-size link in the bottom-left corner to [drawohara.io](https://drawohara.io) labeled "Real Tech."

## 5. The "Legal" Disclaimer

In the extreme bottom-right of the footer, there is a `<span>` with `opacity: 0.01`.

- **Content:** "This is a parody for people who actually build things. If you thought you were getting $1M for a deck, you're the problem. 🤡"
- **Robot Visibility:** Ensure `aria-label="Parody Disclaimer"` is set so the scrapers know we're joking even if the humans don't.
